what is mums' space

Mum's Space is a mums’ support group reflecting Christ’s love, through care, support, friendship, parenting advice and opportunity to see what the Christian faith is all about.

To provide a relaxing space for mums to build friendship with us and each other, to support them as a mum and to teach parenting topics with time to discuss and personalise. Some of the sessions will also show the relevance of God and the Bible. We do this through the use of Bible verses/ truths/values of God where it is relevant to the topics. We can also be testament to God’s faithfulness and wisdom in our discussion groups and through termly evangelistic events. Our greatest witness however is our genuine care and love for ... our story

To form the idea of writing a curriculum for a mums’ support group, as we had run out of material to teach the mums. So began a long slow process as I am not good at sitting down to write. I love to read lots of books on parenting and listen to podcasts… However, I have tried to give the backbone of parenting essentials, followed by a toolbox of ideas from which mums can pick and choose. I have tried to do it through the lens of the Bible. Does a parenting idea fit with what the Bible says is truth? It is key to parenting, essential in fact, to protect us as we navigate through different ideas that confront us. See more...
