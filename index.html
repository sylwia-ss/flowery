what is mums' space

A church based mums' support group reaching into the community. It has proven, in my church, to provide a relaxing space for mums, to build friendship and support with us and each other, and to teach parenting topics with accordance of the Christian faith and termly evangelistic events. our story

I hold Nursing and Health visitor Degree, Biblical and Cross Cultural Diploma, experience as a mum of 4 and volunteering in Mum Ministries. I soon realised how much the mums benefitted from coming together to discuss ideas and concerns of parenting. To untangle the confusing web I read, listened to programmes and podcasts to understand and find solutions. See more...

Parenting Topics

The aim is to provide a 'toolbox' of parenting techniques with underlaying thinking behind them. There is a mass of different opinions on how to parent in our world, hence the aim is to present some of these, sifted, I hope, with wosdom and experience and a Christian starting point. See more...
